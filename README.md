
# Scholar-Arena
Scholar Arena is a gamified study platform that transforms learning into an engaging competition. Students can earn XP, maintain study streaks, climb leaderboards, and challenge themselves through study arenas designed to make academic growth fun and motivating.

 Features (Planned)
- XP and Level System
- Study Streak Tracking
- Global Leaderboards
- Study Challenges and Arenas
- User Profiles
- Achievement Badges
- Premium Features

Scholar Arena is a gamified learning platform designed to transform studying from a daily chore into an exciting and rewarding experience. Instead of simply completing assignments and revising notes, students can earn XP, maintain study streaks, unlock achievements, and climb leaderboards as they progress in their academic journey.
Every study session contributes toward progress in the platform. Users can gain experience points (XP), increase their rank, unlock achievements, and compare their progress with friends and other learners.
The platform aims to create a sense of accomplishment and friendly competition that encourages long-term learning habits.
